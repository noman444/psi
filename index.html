How to Buy?

1- Create MetaMask wallet

Create a meta mask wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/android mobile service. That will allow you to buy, sell, and receive PSI coins.

2- Send ETH to your wallet

Send ETH to your wallet. You can buy Ethereum (ETH) directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Make sure to use the ERC-20 network when transfering ETH.

3- Connect your wallet to Uniswap

Connect your wallet to Uniswap Access your wallet to Uniswap by clicking ‘Connect to a wallet’ and selecting MetaMask.

4- Swap ETH for PSI Coin

Swap ETH for PSI coin You can start swapping as soon as you have ETH available! Press ‘Select a token’ and enter the token address or search for it on the tokens list.
